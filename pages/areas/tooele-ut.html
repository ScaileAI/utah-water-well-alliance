Tooele County Well Considerations

Key facts for Tooele County property owners planning a water well.

  • Tooele Valley Aquifer: A productive basin-fill aquifer serves the Tooele Valley at 150–500 feet. Rush Valley and Skull Valley properties may require deeper drilling into consolidated formations.
  • Water quality: Test for TDS, chloride, and sulfate — common in Tooele County's basin groundwater. Industrial history in some areas warrants a full water quality panel.
  • Rapidly growing area: Water right availability is tightening in some Tooele Valley areas. Applying for your well permit early is recommended, especially for new subdivisions and developments.
