Draper Area Well Considerations

Key facts for Draper property owners planning a water well.

  • Bench vs. valley drilling: Properties on Draper's east bench and in the Traverse Mountains encounter bedrock much sooner than valley floor properties. Consolidated rock drilling is more expensive per foot but can access excellent confined aquifers.
  • County boundary: Some Draper-area properties straddle the Salt Lake/Utah County line. Well permits must be filed with the correct county — our network ensures applications go to the right jurisdiction.
  • High-end residential market: Many Draper bench properties are premium homes. Our network includes drillers experienced with discreet, low-impact operations and well heads that meet HOA and zoning requirements.
