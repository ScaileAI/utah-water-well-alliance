Bountiful & Davis County Well Considerations

Key facts for Bountiful and Davis County property owners planning a water well.

  • Bench deposits: Bountiful's bench is composed of Lake Bonneville delta and terrace deposits. These gravels can yield productive wells at moderate depths, but the underlying consolidated rock requires deeper casing in some areas.
  • Good water quality area: Davis County groundwater generally tests well for drinking quality. Standard testing for coliform, nitrates, and hardness is always recommended for new wells.
  • Drip irrigation demand: Many Davis County fruit and vegetable growers use private irrigation wells to supplement pressurized irrigation systems. Our network handles agricultural and culinary well applications alike.
