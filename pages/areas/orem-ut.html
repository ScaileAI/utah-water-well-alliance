Utah Valley Well Considerations

Key facts for Orem and Utah County property owners planning a water well.

  • Utah Valley Aquifer: The basin-fill aquifer beneath Utah Valley is productive at 150–500 feet, with higher yields near the Provo River and mountain front recharge zones.
  • Water quality: Utah Valley groundwater is generally of good quality. Test for nitrates in agricultural areas and for TDS, hardness, and iron in deeper formations.
  • Water rights in high demand: Utah County's rapid growth means some water right applications face extended review. Early application is key for new construction projects.
