Lehi Area Well Considerations

Key facts for Lehi property owners planning a water well.

  • Valley junction geology: Lehi sits where the Utah Valley and Jordan Valley aquifer systems transition. Depths and yields vary by precise location — certified drillers in our network run site-specific logs before finalizing depth estimates.
  • Rapid growth pressure: Utah County processes a high volume of permit applications due to explosive population growth. Starting your well permit early — before breaking ground — saves weeks on your project timeline.
  • Irrigation & culinary wells: Many Lehi agricultural properties maintain separate irrigation wells and culinary wells. Our network helps with both, and with the water right applications for each use type.
